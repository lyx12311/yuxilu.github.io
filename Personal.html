Personal Life

I was born in Guangzhou, China. I moved to the US in 2014 to attend the University of Maryland (College Park), where I received my bachelor's degree in Physics & Astronomy. Now I live in the best city in the world -- NYC. I am one of the committee members for the AAS Sexual-Orientation & Gender Minorities in Astronomy committee (SGMA) to advocate for LGBTQ+ rights in astronomy. In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, skiing, scuba diving, and biking. I also enjoy visiting art museums, and watching indie movies.
